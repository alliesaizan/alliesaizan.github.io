What makes a comic character successful?

Posted on December 21, 2018

In a previous post, I found that in both Marvel and DC comics, an increasing percentage of new characters are female each year. Further, Marvel has done a better job than DC post-2000 in releasing LGBT characters. However, I wasn’t able to ascertain whether these characteristics impact a character’s popularity. The outcome that answers this question is “number of appearances since character’s release.” Appearance is a measure of popularity; the higher a character’s number of appearances, the more popular s/he has been throughout its tenure.
